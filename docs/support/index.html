Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get verified?

Open the Profile tab and tap "Get verified." Verification helps build trust in the community and is required before you can host trips, join trips, or message other riders.

Why can't I join a trip or send a message?

Joining trips, hosting trips, and messaging require a verified profile. Head to the Profile tab and complete verification to unlock these features.

How do I host a trip?

Tap the Create tab, fill in your trail details, pick a date and difficulty, choose welcome vehicle types, and set a meetup pin on the map. Once posted, riders can request to join.

What is the Recovery / SOS feature?

Recovery lets you broadcast an alert to nearby riders when you need assistance on the trail. It is a community convenience and not a substitute for emergency services. In a real emergency, always call local emergency responders (such as 911) first.

How does the marketplace work?

The Market tab lets you list gear and rigs for sale or post "Wanted" requests. Transactions are arranged directly between buyers and sellers. Always evaluate items carefully and meet safely.

How do I enable location and notifications?

Location powers nearby rider discovery, trip meetups, and recovery alerts. Notifications deliver trip reminders and recovery broadcasts. You can manage both anytime in your device's Settings under OffroadMate.

How do I edit my profile or change my rig?

Go to the Profile tab and tap "Edit profile" to update your name, location, vehicle, rig details, skill level, bio, and photo.

How do I delete my account?

To request account and data deletion, email us at jperez845@outlook.com from the address associated with your account and we'll process your request.
